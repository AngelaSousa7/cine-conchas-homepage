Venue

Quinta das Conchas is one of Lisbon’s most beautiful parks. We offer our visitors a snack area, playground, forest paths with great conditions for the practice of cross-country and use of bicycles.
Regarding our event, we have noise related strict rules. So don’t worry: come and enjoy with your loved ones.

Accessibility

In Quinta das Conchas we welcome everyone. Our facilities allow wheelchairs.
